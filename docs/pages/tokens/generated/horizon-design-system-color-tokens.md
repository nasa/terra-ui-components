# Horizon Design System Color Tokens

The Horizon Design System colors are organized into a primary palette, an extended palette, and a set of neutrals. The primary palette consists of the core brand colors, while the extended palette includes tints and shades of those colors for added flexibility. The neutrals provide a range of values for text and backgrounds to ensure appropriate contrast across the system.

### Primary Palette

Our primary palette consists of black, white, red, and blue. These colors are used in logical ways throughout the site to highlight actions when they are important. We use a restricted palette to give attention and hierarchy to our content without distraction.
