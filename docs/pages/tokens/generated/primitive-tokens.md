# Primitive Tokens

Primitive tokens are the most basic level of tokens in the design system. Primitive tokens are expanded color palettes of the <a href="/tokens/generated/horizon-design-system-color-tokens">Horizon Design System</a> colors. Shade 500 from each palette equates to the base HDS color. A wider selection of color shades is needed to represent component states, and to ensure there are enough options to maintain accessible color combinations across the system. The primitive tokens are organized by color family (e.g. red, blue, neutral) and by hue/saturation/value. The primitive tokens are used directly in the <a href="/tokens/generated/foundation-tokens">Foundation tokens</a> which define the use of color based on roles.
